Introduction

Nepal is a beautiful country located in South Asia, known for its majestic Himalayan mountains, diverse culture, and warm hospitality.

Culture

Nepal has a rich cultural heritage influenced by Hinduism and Buddhism. Festivals like Dashain, Tihar, and Holi are celebrated with great enthusiasm.

Tourism

Tourists from around the world visit Nepal for trekking, mountaineering, and exploring UNESCO World Heritage Sites such as Kathmandu Valley, Lumbini, and Chitwan National Park.
